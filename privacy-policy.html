Overview

The Holy Quran: Indexed is provided by Arfhause. The app is designed to help users read, search, and navigate Quran content. We aim to collect only the information that is necessary to provide and improve the app.

This policy is the governing privacy policy for The Holy Quran: Indexed and applies to the app, its related services, and support requests sent to Arfhause.

Information We Handle

The app does not require users to create an account, and Arfhause does not ask users to provide a name, email address, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, payment information, or government identifiers in order to use the app.

Information stored on your device

App preferences, reading progress, bookmarks, favorites, notes, search history, or similar app settings may be stored locally on your device when you use features that need them. This information is used to provide the app experience and is not sold by Arfhause.

Automatically processed technical information

Depending on your device, operating system, and distribution channel, limited technical information may be processed to operate, secure, diagnose, or improve the app. This may include device type, operating system version, app version, language settings, crash logs, performance information, approximate region derived from network information, and similar diagnostic data.

If the app uses any third-party analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or cloud service in a future version, this policy and the Google Play Data safety section should be updated so they remain accurate and consistent.

How Information Is Used

Information handled by the app is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing Quran reading, search, indexing, navigation, and personalization features.
  • Saving local preferences such as bookmarks, progress, or display settings.
  • Maintaining app reliability, diagnosing crashes, and improving performance.
  • Responding to user support, privacy, or deletion requests.
  • Complying with legal obligations and protecting the app, users, and developer account.

Sharing and Third Parties

Arfhause does not sell personal or sensitive user data. We may share limited information only when necessary to operate the app, comply with law, protect rights and safety, or work with trusted service providers that help deliver app services.

Some app distribution, security, crash reporting, analytics, or platform services may be provided by third parties such as Google Play services. Their handling of information is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information handled by the app. Where information is transmitted over a network by the app or supporting services, it should be transmitted using modern secure transport such as HTTPS.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work to reduce risk and limit information handling to appropriate app and service purposes.

Data Retention and Deletion

Information stored locally on your device remains there until you delete it inside the app, clear the app data, or uninstall the app. Support requests and related correspondence may be retained for as long as needed to respond, maintain records, resolve disputes, or comply with legal obligations.

To request deletion of information associated with a support request, contact us at arfhause@gmail.com. If the app later offers account creation or cloud sync, we will provide an account deletion method and update this policy accordingly.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience. Arfhause does not knowingly request or collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support channel, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date on this page. Continued use of the app after an update means the revised policy applies from that date onward.

Contact

If you have questions, privacy requests, or concerns about this policy, contact Arfhause at arfhause@gmail.com.
